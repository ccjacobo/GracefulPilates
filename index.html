About Grace

Grace is a certified Pilates instructor known for focused flows and precise, hands‑on coaching. Her sessions pair curated playlists with mindful sequencing, guiding clients to feel exactly which muscles are working and why. Expect a supportive, confidence‑building atmosphere — and a deeper connection to your body’s mechanics.
